Sword Art Online

It is an anime based on the Kawahara River light novel series.

Sword Art Online

The action takes place in a relatively near future, 2022, when the virtual world has become so high-quality and sophisticated that it has become almost impossible to distinguish it from the real one.

Sword Art Online

You can get into a parallel reality with the help of a special helmet NerveGear, which simulates all human feelings and transfers them to the virtual world. All in all, NerveGear is such a well-tuned Oculus Rift.

KIRITO

The main character is Katsuto aka Kirito (nick in the game). He is one of the beta testers, therefore, at the time of the public launch of Sword Art Online, Katsuto's character is several levels ahead of the rest of the players. Kirito is a swordsman (to the question of the title of the series) and an eternal loner. For almost the entire game, he keeps on by himself, only occasionally joining guilds. Kirito is also a bit of a womanizer: almost every chan he meets during the game falls in love with him.According to Asuna, Kirito is quite straightforward (possibly due to his small number of friends and his reclusive lifestyle).

Naruto

"Naruto" is a shonen manga by Masashi Kishimoto.

Naruto

Which tells about the life of a noisy and restless teenage ninja Naruto Uzumaki.

Naruto
